Halloween Door

This door was designed by the 3rd A/C Bilingual Section class for the Halloween Door Design Competition, run by the English department, with the goal of creating a Halloween decoration for the school. It depicts an old, abandoned ticket booth where a creepy clown is selling tickets to a show. We tried to use as many recycled items as possible, from cardboard from old boxes to unusable gloves.

Recycled materials used

  • Thick cardboard boxes for the box office and bars.
  • Reused paper and cardboard for the tickets, the clown, and other details like the lights.
  • Latex cleaning gloves.
  • Acrylic paint we had at home.

Design

The clown has a special meaning. The cracks and bars show the passage of time, and the gloves and knife suggest danger.
